Valley Fold ⌄
Fold toward you — V shape. Most common.
Mountain Fold ⌃
Fold away — peak up. Valley geometry, flipped.
Crease & Unfold ◇
Fold then unfold — leaves a reference line.
Pleat Fold ≡
Valley + mountain — zigzag accordion ridge.
Petal Fold
Opens flap into two symmetric points.
Squash Fold
Open a layered flap and press it flat.
Rabbit Ear
Three creases form a single pointed ear.
Inside Reverse
Reverses a point inward between layers.
Outside Reverse
Reverses a point outward over the layers.
Sink Fold
Pushes a corner inside the model.
